Curriculum Vitae

Research interests:

  • My research interest mainly lies in the fields of ice dynamics and its interaction with, e.g., polar meteorology, climate and ocean circulation. In particular, I am interested in the thermo-mechanical behaviors of (marine) ice sheet, e.g., the grounding line dynamics, and also the dynamical changes of alpine glaciers in Tibet.
